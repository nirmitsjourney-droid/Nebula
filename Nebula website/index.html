Questions

Questions you might ask

Everything you need to know before giving Nebula a try.

All processing happens on your device first. When cloud assistance is required, your data is end-to-end encrypted with keys only you hold. We have no servers that can read your content, no telemetry, no third-party trackers. You can audit the open-source core anytime.

macOS 12+, Windows 10+, Linux (Ubuntu, Fedora, Arch), iOS 16+, Android 12+, and a Progressive Web App for everything else. Raspberry Pi and home-server binaries are available for self-hosting enthusiasts.

Yes. The core assistant, scheduling, knowledge base, and smart-home integrations run entirely offline. Cloud-powered features (web search, large document summarization) gracefully degrade when offline and queue actions for when you reconnect.

General AI chatbots wait for you to ask. Nebula proactively orchestrates your real environment — files, calendar, email, smart home, code repos — and acts on your behalf with your permission. It's an agent, not a chat box.

Yes — no contracts, no exit fees. Cancel from your account settings in one click. You'll keep Pro features until the end of your billing period, then automatically drop to the free Starter plan. Your data stays yours; export it as a structured archive anytime.
